How to Use the AI Tax Advisor

  • Step 1: Enter Email: Use the email field to save your progress or load a previously saved profile.
  • Step 2: Fill Profile: Open the "Your Profile" section and enter your age and residential status.
  • Step 3: Fill Income: Open the "Your Income" section and fill in all your income sources.
  • Step 4: Fill Deductions: Open the "Your Savings" section and enter all applicable deductions and investments.
  • Step 5: Analyze: Click the "Save & Analyze" button to get your detailed tax report and personalized advice.
